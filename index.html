ANINDYO HOBBY CLUB

The best way to connect people with your hobby.

Painting Exebition

We arrange painting or drawing exebition programme twice a year in our club. Our drawing lover artists draw the pictures and wehang the selected pictures for the exebition. We invite the famous artists as our chief guest. They visit our club & announce the best pictue.

Programming Contest

Some of our club members are also interested in ICT sectors. They like to do programming, creating animated cartoon and interested to learn programming languages. To make them more interested in this sector, we arrange programming contest in our club.

Cultural Competetion

We also arrange cultural programme twice a year in our club. Our club members who are intereste in song, dance, reciting poem or acting, they present their performence in the stage before the guests & audience. Then the judges announce the winner's name for each sector and distribute prizes among the winners.

Hi! This is Sorifa Alam

I am from Tongi-Gazipur & belonging to a nuclear family. I have passed my SSC and HSC from Safiuddin Sarker Academy and college. Currently I am a student of CIS department, Daffodil International University.

I love sharing my work with the world. Knowing that I am creating for an audience gives each of my projects an additional boost. Although being so public is not always easy. I've found the feedback and comments. I hope, you will enjoy browsing my latest project & get in touch for feedback or possible collaborations.

My Hobby
